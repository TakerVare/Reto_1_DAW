Meet the minds behind BurWeb

At Burweb, we're more than just burger lovers — we're a passionate team committed to blending great food with great digital experiences. Meet the minds behind the brand:

Daniel – CEO & Co-Founder

Daniel is the strategic engine of Burweb. With a strong background in entrepreneurship and business development, he oversees the company's long-term vision, brand direction, and growth strategy. Daniel's leadership style combines innovation with practicality, ensuring Burweb stays ahead in both customer satisfaction and operational excellence. His passion for food culture and digital transformation is the heart of everything we do.

Guillermo – CTO & Co-Founder

Guillermo leads all things tech at Burweb. As our Chief Technology Officer, he is responsible for building and maintaining our digital platforms, including our online ordering system and internal tools. With years of experience in full-stack development and system architecture, Guillermo ensures that our technology is scalable, secure, and user-friendly. His problem-solving mindset and love for clean code make him a cornerstone of our tech-driven approach.

Gabriel – Head of Operations

Gabriel is the bridge between the digital world and our on-the-ground restaurant operations. With a strong foundation in logistics and customer service management, he ensures every Burweb location runs smoothly and meets the highest quality standards. Gabriel focuses on optimizing daily processes, training teams, and making sure the customer experience is consistent across all touchpoints. His hands-on approach and attention to detail keep the business running like clockwork.
